# **Obsidian中的标签和链接 - 笔记组织指南 (Guide to Tags and Links - Note Organization in Obsidian)**

---

## **1. 笔记类型标签 (Note Type Tags)**

标识笔记的主要性质，有助于基本内容分类。

Identify the primary nature of the note, helping with basic content categorization.

### **示例 (Examples):**

- `#type/task` → 待办事项
- `#type/project` → 开发中的项目
- `#type/daily` → 日记
- `#type/study` → 学习材料
- `#type/idea` → 想法和头脑风暴
- `#type/reflection` → 个人思考和分析
- `#type/habits` → 日常惯例和习惯

To-do items, Projects in development, Daily notes, Learning material, Insights and brainstorming, Personal thoughts and analysis, Routines and habits

---

## **2. 上下文标签 (Context Tags)**

指示笔记适用的环境或情况。

Indicate the environment or circumstance where the note applies.

### **示例 (Examples):**

- `#context/studies` → 一般学习材料
- `#context/work` → 职业活动
- `#context/personal` → 个人发展
- `#context/academic` → 大学学习
- `#context/hobbies` → 娱乐活动

General learning material, Professional activities, Personal development, University studies, Recreational activities

## **5. 标签使用最佳实践 (Tag Usage Best Practices)**

#### **必需的最低标签 (Required Minimum Tags)**

为了保持一致性并便于搜索，每个笔记至少应包含：

To maintain consistency and facilitate search, each note should include at minimum:

1. 一个类型标签 (`#type/...`)
2. 一个上下文标签 (`#context/...`)
3. 一个主题标签 (`#theme/...`)
4. 适用时的状态/优先级标签

One type tag (`#type/...`)
One context tag (`#context/...`)
One theme tag (`#theme/...`)
One status/priority tag when applicable

## **6. 使用链接 (Using Links)**

链接是创建笔记之间连接的基础，形成相互连接的知识网络。

Links are fundamental for creating connections between notes, forming an interconnected knowledge network.

### **链接类型 (Link Types)**

**1. 直接链接 (Direct Links)**
   - 语法：`[[Note Name]]`
   - 用途：直接连接到另一个笔记
   - 示例：`[[Cryptography Algorithms]]`
   ---
   - Syntax: `[[Note Name]]`
   - Usage: Directly connects to another note
   - Example: `[[Cryptography Algorithms]]`

**2. 别名链接 (Alias Links)**
   - 语法：`[[Note Name|Displayed Text]]`
   - 用途：显示与笔记标题不同的文本
   - 示例：`[[Cryptography Algorithms|Encryption Methods]]`
   ---
   - Syntax: `[[Note Name|Displayed Text]]`
   - Usage: Shows different text from the note title
   - Example: `[[Cryptography Algorithms|Encryption Methods]]`

**3. 章节链接 (Section Links)**
   - 语法：`[[Note Name#Section]]`
   - 用途：带你到笔记的特定章节
   - 示例：`[[Cryptography#RSA]]`
   ---
   - Syntax: `[[Note Name#Section]]`
   - Usage: Takes you to a specific section of a note
   - Example: `[[Cryptography#RSA]]`

### **推荐的链接实践 (Recommended Link Practices)**

**1. 有意义的连接 (Meaningful Connections)**
   - 只在存在相关关系时创建链接
   - 避免可能造成干扰的过多链接
   ---
   - Create links only when there's a relevant relationship
   - Avoid excessive links that might distract

**2. 索引笔记 (MOCs) (Index Notes)**
   - 创建作为内容地图的笔记
   - 使用链接组织相关主题
   - 示例：一个"[[Security Projects]]"笔记，包含所有相关项目的链接
   ---
   - Create notes that serve as maps of content
   - Use links to organize related themes
   - Example: A "[[Security Projects]]" note with links to all related projects

**3. 双向链接 (Bidirectional Links)**
   - 利用 Obsidian 的自动反向链接
   - 定期查看反向链接以发现新连接
   ---
   - Take advantage of Obsidian's automatic backlinks
   - Periodically review backlinks to discover new connections

**4. 通过链接创建层次结构 (Hierarchy through Links)**
   - 使用链接创建灵活的层次结构
   - 示例：`[[Programming]]` → `[[Algorithms]]` → `[[Sorting]]`
   ---
   - Use links to create flexible hierarchical structures
   - Example: `[[Programming]]` → `[[Algorithms]]` → `[[Sorting]]`
